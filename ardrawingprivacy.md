This Privacy Policy describes how AR Drawing Anime: Trace Sketch ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use
Camera Access
The App requires access to your device's camera to enable the Core AR Drawing and Tracing features. The camera feed is used in real-time to overlay drawing templates on your physical drawing surface. We do not store, upload, or share your camera feed or any images captured through the camera on our servers.

Microphone Access
The App requests microphone access (RECORD_AUDIO) if you use features related to recording your drawing process (e.g., time-lapse or video recording). Audio data is processed locally on your device and is not collected by us.

Storage Access
The App requires access to your device's storage to:

Load images from your gallery for tracing.
Save your completed drawings or recorded videos to your device. Data accessed from your storage remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
