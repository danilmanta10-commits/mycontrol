# Privacy Policy for Clap to Find Phone

**Last Updated:** January 16, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Clap to Find Phone ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

#### Microphone Audio Data
- **Purpose:** To detect clapping sounds for the phone-finding feature
- **Processing:** Audio is processed locally on your device in real-time
- **Storage:** Audio data is NOT recorded, stored, or transmitted. Only the detection of clap patterns is processed
- **Usage:** Used solely to trigger alerts (sound, vibration, flashlight) when a double-clap is detected

#### Device Permissions
The App requests the following permissions:

1. **Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)** - Required to listen for clap sounds
2. **Camera (CAMERA)** - Used to control the flashlight feature
3. **Vibration (VIBRATE)** - Used to trigger vibration alerts
4. **Internet (INTERNET)** - Used to display advertisements
5. **Modify Audio Settings (MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS)** - Used to control alert sound volume
6. **Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK)** - Keeps the app running in the background to detect claps
7. **Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE)** - Allows continuous clap detection
8. **Read External Storage (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)** - For accessing custom sound files (Android 12 and below)

### Information Stored Locally

The following preferences are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences:

- App activation status (on/off)
- Selected alert sound
- Sound volume level
- Sound duration
- Vibration pattern preference
- Flashlight mode preference
- Sensitivity settings
- Language preference

**Important:** This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality
- **Clap Detection:** Audio input is analyzed in real-time to detect double-clap patterns
- **Alert Triggering:** When a clap is detected, the app triggers your chosen alerts (sound, vibration, flashlight)
- **User Preferences:** Your settings are saved locally to provide a personalized experience

## Technical Information

### Background Service
The App runs a foreground service to continuously listen for claps when activated. This service:
- Displays a persistent notification while active
- Uses minimal battery through optimized audio processing
- Can be stopped at any time by deactivating the app

### Audio Processing
- **Sample Rate:** 44,100 Hz
- **Processing:** Real-time analysis for clap signature detection
- **No Recording:** Audio is analyzed in memory buffers and immediately discarded
- **No Transmission:** Audio data never leaves your device
